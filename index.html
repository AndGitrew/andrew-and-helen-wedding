Our Love Story...

If we had to tell you one thing about us... it would be that we're so thankful. Thankful that the stars aligned as they did. In life you interact with a lot of
different people, but only once in a lifetime do you find someone who is your perfect match. We're so excited to be getting married and most of all
excited to share our lives with one another. We are also grateful for the support of our family and friends over the past two years and feel blessed
to have you all in our lives. We look forward to sharing this special moment with you!

2015

05 September: He saw Her

The first night they met was at Chad and Erin Specker's wedding, in Campbellford, Ontario.

Andrew's best friend Marc noticed that Helen had an eye for Andrew on the dance floor and insisted that he slow dance with her. One country song later and the rest is history...

01 October: Officially Dating!

Since Helen wanted Andrew to officially ask her to be his girlfriend, he obviously had to think of a romantic way of doing it. So he decided to make her a song entitled "True Love" in which he asked her to be his girlfriend.

08 October: "I Ruv you"

During a casual conversation Andrew awkwardly mumbled an "I ruv you" to Helen. That's right - "I ruv you". She then cleverly asked him what it meant so he would say the real thing. So he worked up the courage to say those incredible three words - "I love you".

2016

12 August: She moved in

A little under a year after they met, Andrew and Helen decided to move in together. After several discussions, they thought it made perfect sense given the fact that they had been in a long distance relationship, with Andrew living in Gatineau and Helen living in Toronto.

12 September: She said yes!

So how did Andrew pop the question? At the place where they first met! That's right - Campbellford, Ontario. Andrew knew they would approach the exit as they were on their way back from Charlie and Tina Costin's wedding in Hamilton. Helen was very sick with a cold but Andrew had already planned to propose and wasn't about to let a microscopic virus get between him and his destiny. After he finally managed to convince her to detour and take a trip down memory lane - BAM! - he proposed.
